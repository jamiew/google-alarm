Google Alarm

Get notified when Google is monitoring your web browsing

The Google Alarm Firefox addon visually & audibly alerts you when your personal information is being sent to Google [...].

Even if you're not on Gmail or YouTube you might still be sending data to Google through their vast network of "tracking bugs" – Google Analytics, Google AdSense, YouTube embeds... these all your send personal information back to Google HQ, and can be used to monitor your web browsing habits. Stay alert! Install Google Alarm today.
