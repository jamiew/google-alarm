Double Rainbow

Double Rainbow is a viral video featuring YouTube user HungryBear9562 proudly exclaiming his awe while witnessing a rare double rainbow. Some notable quotes from the video include “it’s so bright and vivid”, “it’s starting to look like a triple rainbow”, and “what does it mean?”.
